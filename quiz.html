What your Rat Terrier wants YOU to know!

Rat Terriers are not overly furry dogs, and Ratty's from California and the southern US have even less hair. Consider this when you move them up to Canada or anywhere in the PNW. It's cold here, and wet and dark. For me, somebody who can't stand the culture of dressing pets, it was a big pill to swallow when I had to buy sweaters and a rain coat, but as Django wants to tell you - he gets COLD. And he does not like the rain and the way it coldly dribbles down his back and in his ears late at night. So, get over to Chilly sweaters and get shopping!

More Excellent Owner Info!

Dogs, especially rescue dogs and especially Rat Terrier rescue dogs can't tell you much. So with Django sitting here beside me I will translate as best I can, some things that Rat Terriers, especially rescued Rat terriers want you to know.Django has been with us for six weeks now and he is just starting to find his voice, so these are the things he would like you to know, and consider before you think about getting a Rat Terrier, and especially a rescue Ratty.

A rescued Rat terrier is just like a puppy, it will need to be taught, and taught kindly, with lots of positive reinforcement. Did you know that some dogs who have been caged, do not even know how to go up and down stairs? It is hard for us to imagine, but for them it is a reality, so you must have patience. You will need to teach them how to sit, come, stay etc. all through positive reinforcement. Never yell at a rescue, they will just shut and in confusion and hurt.
